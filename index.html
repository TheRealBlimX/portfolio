All the King's Men

This game was created using Javascript and a compiler called Perlenspiel 2.0. Using the directional buttons, players control the King as he tries to position his lousy servants (the pawns) and horses (knights) into their correct positions. This puzzle game requires players to move pawns and knights into their respective squares in order to advance to the next part of the castle...and eventually bed!
